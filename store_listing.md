## Full Description (EN)

### Track the Skies in Real Time

AirWatch is a next-generation flight tracking app that displays thousands of aircraft on an interactive world map. With a sleek radar-inspired design and real-time data from aviation networks, AirWatch gives you complete visibility into the world's airspace.

### Features

**Live Flight Tracking**
• Track 7,000+ aircraft worldwide in real time
• See flight routes, altitude, speed, and heading
• Tap any aircraft for detailed flight information
• Real aircraft photos with airline livery

**Comprehensive Flight Data**
• Airline name, flight number, and aircraft type
• Departure and arrival airports with city names
• Gate, terminal, and baggage information
• Flight status: en-route, landed, scheduled, delayed
• Delay information with scheduled vs actual times

**Smart Search**
• Search by flight number, airline, or registration
• Autocomplete suggestions as you type
• Filter by jets, helicopters, cargo, or light aircraft
• Find scheduled and landed flights, not just live ones

**Airport Information**
• Tap any airport on the map for detailed info
• Live weather: temperature, wind, humidity
• Real-time departure and arrival boards
• 150+ major airports marked worldwide

**Flight History**
• View 7-day flight history for any route
• Track delays and on-time performance
• Cross-reference multiple data sources

**Personalization**
• Save favorite flights, airlines, and airports
• Choose between dark, light, and satellite map themes
• Customize units: feet/meters, knots/km/h/mph
• Available in English, German, and French

### Data Coverage
• 6,244 airlines worldwide
• 20,845 airports worldwide
• Data from multiple aviation sources
